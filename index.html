About POLARIS

POLARIS is an AI-powered university admissions companion designed to help students build stronger applications and make informed decisions about higher education.

The app provides personalized university recommendations, application planning, academic progress tracking, resume analysis, and admissions guidance using Apple's Foundation Models where available.

Your data remains under your control. POLARIS is designed with privacy in mind and stores your information locally on your device whenever possible.

Features

  • Personalized university recommendations
  • AI admissions advisor
  • Resume analysis
  • Progress tracking
  • Application planning
  • On-device AI with Apple Intelligence where supported
  • Privacy-focused design
