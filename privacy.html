Introduction

Thank you for using POLARIS.

POLARIS is designed to help students organize their academic profile, receive personalized university guidance, and plan their higher education journey while respecting user privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information POLARIS collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information You Provide

POLARIS allows you to voluntarily enter information including:

  • Your name
  • School
  • Country
  • Academic curriculum
  • Grades and standardized test scores
  • Intended major
  • Dream universities
  • Activities and achievements
  • Skills, certifications, languages, and awards
  • Conversation history with the AI Advisor
  • Resume files that you choose to import

You are responsible for ensuring that the information you provide is accurate.

Resume Import

If you choose to import a resume, POLARIS extracts text from the document to populate your academic profile and personalize recommendations.

Resume import only occurs after you explicitly choose a file using Apple's document picker.

How Your Information Is Used

Information you provide is used to:

  • Personalize university recommendations
  • Evaluate your application profile
  • Provide personalized AI guidance
  • Track your progress over time

POLARIS does not use your information for advertising.

Apple Intelligence & AI Processing

POLARIS uses Apple's Foundation Models and Apple Intelligence where supported by your device.

Depending on your device, settings, and the capabilities required for a request, AI processing may occur either on your device or through Apple's Private Cloud Compute, which is designed to protect user privacy.

Data Storage

Your information is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

POLARIS does not maintain user accounts or store your personal information on developer-operated servers.

If you delete the app or choose Reset All Data, your locally stored information will be removed.

Data Sharing

POLARIS does not sell your personal information or share it with advertisers.

POLARIS does not store your personal information on developer-operated servers. AI features rely on Apple's Foundation Models and may process requests on your device or, when necessary, through Apple's Private Cloud Compute according to Apple's privacy protections.

Information is only shared when required for Apple platform functionality or when required by law.

Third-Party Technologies

POLARIS relies on Apple technologies, including:

  • SwiftData
  • PDFKit
  • Apple Foundation Models
  • Apple Intelligence
  • Apple Private Cloud Compute (when required)
  • Apple Document Picker

These technologies operate according to Apple's own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

POLARIS is intended for students planning higher education.

Users below the age required by local law should use the application under the supervision of a parent or guardian where appropriate.

Security

POLARIS is designed to minimize data collection and relies on Apple's platform security features.

While reasonable measures are taken to protect your information, no software can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • Update your information at any time.
  • Import or replace your resume.
  • Delete all locally stored information using the Reset All Data feature.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.
