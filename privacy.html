Introduction

Thank you for using POLARIS.

POLARIS helps students organize their academic profile, receive personalized university guidance, and plan their higher education journey while respecting user privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information POLARIS collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information You Provide

POLARIS allows you to voluntarily enter information including:

  • Your name
  • School and country
  • Academic curriculum
  • Grades and standardized test scores
  • Intended major
  • Dream universities
  • Activities and achievements
  • Skills, certifications, languages, and awards
  • Conversation history with the AI Advisor
  • Resume files you choose to import

Resume Import

If you choose to import a resume, POLARIS extracts text from the document to populate your profile and personalize recommendations.

Resume import only occurs after you explicitly select a file using Apple's document picker.

How Your Information Is Used

  • Personalize university recommendations
  • Evaluate your application profile
  • Generate admissions guidance
  • Track your progress
  • Improve AI conversations

POLARIS does not use your information for advertising.

On-Device AI

POLARIS uses Apple's Foundation Models and Apple Intelligence where supported by your device.

When available, AI processing occurs locally on your device.

Data Storage

Your information is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

POLARIS does not maintain user accounts or permanently store your personal information on developer-operated servers.

If you delete the app or use the Reset All Data feature, your locally stored information is removed.

Data Sharing

POLARIS does not sell your personal information.

POLARIS does not share your information with advertisers.

Information is only shared when required for Apple platform functionality or if required by law.

Third-Party Technologies

  • Apple Foundation Models
  • SwiftData
  • PDFKit
  • Apple Document Picker

Your Choices

  • Update your profile at any time
  • Replace your resume whenever you wish
  • Delete all local data using Reset All Data
